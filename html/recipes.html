Garlic Sauce

  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp ground chili paste or red pepper flakes
  • 2 tsp or 4 cloves garlic finely chopped
  • 1.2 tsp salt

Combine and cook (will reduce by 1/2). Allow to cool to room temperature before serving. May store for two months in a closed jar. If it is too thick you can add more water.

Chili Oil

  • 4 Tbls red pepper flakes
  • 1 cup oil
    • Bring almost to a boil over medium heat. Let cool. Strain into jar with lid.
